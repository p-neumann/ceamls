NSF Workshop on Interdisciplinary and Collaborative Research in Cybersecurity
Columbus, Georgia
In today's increasingly networked, distributed, and asynchronous world, cybersecurity involves hardware, software, networks, data, people, and integration with the physical world. NSF has long recognized the value of interdisciplinary research in pushing fields forward and accelerating scientific discovery. Thus, NSF gives high priority to promoting interdisciplinary research and supports it through a number of specific solicitations. NSF also encourages researchers to submit unsolicited interdisciplinary proposals for ideas that are in novel or emerging areas extending beyond any particular current NSF program. The aim of this workshop is to bring together researchers and industry partners from different disciplines discussing emerging issues and research topics in cybersecurity.
